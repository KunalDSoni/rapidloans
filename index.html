◈ What We Do

Comprehensive Financial Services

From origination to servicing, we cover the entire lending lifecycle with technology at the core.

📄

Microloan Origination

End-to-end digital loan origination for micro and small loans, powered by AI-driven credit assessment and instant KYC verification.

🏦

Banking Correspondent

Acting as a technology-enabled extension of banks and NBFCs, bringing formal financial services directly to rural and semi-urban doorsteps.

💰

Collection & Servicing

Automated collection management with real-time tracking, digital payment options, and intelligent follow-up workflows for borrowers.

👥

Group Lending

Community-based lending programs designed for self-help groups and joint liability groups, fostering financial inclusion at grassroot levels.

📊

Credit Assessment

Proprietary scoring models that go beyond traditional credit bureaus — leveraging alternative data to assess creditworthiness of first-time borrowers.

📱

Digital Payments

Seamless UPI, NACH, and e-mandate integrations enabling borrowers to make repayments effortlessly from anywhere using their mobile devices.
